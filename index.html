The Library

This exhibition features material from the National Library of Australia's collection about the Library's building. The story of the building forms part of the Library's onsite exhibition '1968: Changing Times' (1 March - 12 August 2018), which also looks at important national and international events from 1968. In this online exhibition we highlight some important collection material concering the design and building of the lakeside building, which opened in 1968.
